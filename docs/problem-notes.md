# Problem Notes

## Target Users

* International students who are new to the university.
* Exchange students and first-year international students.
* Students who need information about dormitories, cafeterias, and campus life before arriving.

## Incidents

* New students are unsure which dormitory to choose.
* Students do not know which cafeterias offer affordable or suitable food.
* Students arrive on campus without knowing what facilities are available.
* Students spend time searching through multiple websites, social media pages, and group chats for information.
* Students receive conflicting advice from different sources.

## Pain Points

* Difficulty finding trustworthy information about dormitories.
* Lack of honest student reviews about accommodation and food.
* Information is scattered across many platforms.
* Official university websites may not provide detailed student experiences.
* Students feel overwhelmed when adapting to a new environment.

## Current Alternatives

* University websites
* Google searches
* Facebook groups
* LINE group chats
* Asking friends or senior students
* Student forums and social media

## Consequences

* Students may choose unsuitable accommodation.
* Students may spend more money than necessary on housing or food.
* Students waste time searching for information.
* Students experience stress and uncertainty during their transition to university.
* Students may miss useful campus resources and services because they are unaware of them.
